How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?
Having that intention in mind during this part of the sprint acutally helped me to focus on first setting up all the sections first then playing around later with the getting the details right. I found this process much eaiser to go through knowing that I don't need to have it perfect, it also helped me move through the task a lot quicker knowing that I style and such can be tweaked once each part is up in its place.

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?
Each part of that chapter I feel like I knew, and not just as a concept, but from personal experience. Meditation is powerful tool that ultimalty brings awareness to oneself and I beleive that awarenes is the key to living a happy and enjoyable life, regardless of who you are and what you are doing in life.
