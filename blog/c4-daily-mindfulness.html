Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?
I didnt have anyone to practice this with formally, so I did it informally with family members during dinner when usually I would engage in conversation, but this tie I just listened and allowed for them to speak and it was very amazing to see what coming up for me when I would usually just react to or have something to say immediately, I just allowed them to talk the conversation changed.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?
All of them. The listening I feel is an important part of my day to day life, being able to listen to how I am feeling, how others around me are feeling, and also giving space to allow for what arises to be seen and acknowledged, instead of just pushing it away.
